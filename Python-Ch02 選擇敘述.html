2-1 關係運算子

Python的輸出函式為"print()"，結果會顯示於console上

表2-1 關係運算子
運算子 意義
< 小於
<= 小於等於
> 大於
>= 大於等於
== 等於
!= 不等於

2-2 if 敘述

語法:

                    
                    if 條件運算式:
                        主體敘述

input()函釋回傳的結果是一個字串，而括號裡面可以放任何想在裡面顯式的訊號，例如:

範例程式

                    
                    a = eval(input('Enter a number: '))
                    if a > 0:
                        print(a, 'is greater than 0')
                    print('Over')

輸出結果:

                    
                        Enter a number: 100
                        100 is great than 
                        Over

2-3 if...else 敘述

語法:

                    
                    if 條件運算式:
                        主體敘述 1
                    else:
                        主體敘述 2

敘述

範例程式

                    
                    a = eval(input('Enter a number: '))
                    if a > 0:
                        print(a, 'is greater than 0')
                    else:
                        print(a, 'is less than 0')
                    print('Over')

輸出結果:

(一)

                    
                        Enter a number: 100
                        100 is great than 
                        Over

(二)

                    
                        Enter a number: -100
                        -100 is less than 
                        Over

2-4 if...elif...else 敘述

語法:

                    
                    if 條件運算式:
                        主體敘述 1
                    elif條件運算式:
                        主體敘述 2
                    eles:
                        主體敘述3

敘述

範例程式

                    
                    a = eval(input('Enter a number: '))
                    if a > 0:
                        print(a, 'is greater than 0')
                    elif a < 0:
                        print(a, 'is less than 0')
                    else:
                        print(a, 'is eaual to 0')
                    print('Over')

輸出結果:

(一)

                    
                        Enter a number: 100
                        100 is great than 0
                        Over

(二)

                    
                        Enter a number: -100
                        -100 is less than 0
                        Over

(三)

                    
                        Enter a number: 0
                        0 is equal to 0 
                        Over

2-5 邏輯運算子

表2-3 邏輯運算子
運算子 意義
and 且
or 或
not 反

敘述

範例程式

                    
                        num = eval(input('Enter a number: '))
                        if (85<=num) and (num <= 95):
                            print('%d is in the between 85 and 95'%(num))
                        else:
                            print('%d is not in the between 85 and 95'%(num))
                        print('Over')

輸出結果:

(一)

                    
                        Enter a number: 100
                        100 is not in the between 85 and 95 
                        Over

(二)

                    
                        Enter a number: 90
                        90 is in the between 85 and 95 
                        Over

其實，也可以這樣改寫

範例程式

                    
                        num = eval(input('Enter a number: '))
                        if (85 <= num <= 95):
                            print('%d is in the between 85 and 95'%(num))
                        else:
                            print('%d is not in the between 85 and 95'%(num))
                        print('Over')

輸出結果:

(一)

                    
                        Enter a number: 100
                        100 is not in the between 85 and 95 
                        Over

(二)

                    
                        Enter a number: 90
                        90 is in the between 85 and 95 
                        Over
